### Xamarin.Forms WebView
HTML5 video does not work with [Xamarin.Forms.WebView](https://docs.microsoft.com/ko-kr/dotnet/api/xamarin.forms.webview?view=xamarin-forms) on Samsung Smart TV due to security policy.
